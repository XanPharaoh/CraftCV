CraftCV – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 24, 2026

CraftCV ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Resume Content: Text you paste or upload, used solely to tailor your resume. Not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Job Description: Text you enter, used solely for AI tailoring. Not stored permanently.
  • Device ID: A randomly generated anonymous ID used to manage free-tier usage limits. Not linked to your identity.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous request counts to enforce fair usage limits.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Precise location
  • Contacts, photos, or files beyond what you explicitly upload
  • Browsing history or other apps

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To process your resume and generate tailored content using AI
  • To enforce free-tier usage limits via your anonymous device ID
  • To improve app performance and reliability

4. Third-Party Services

CraftCV uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing: Handles all subscription payments. We never see or store your payment details. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: Serves ads to free-tier users. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers. Google Privacy Policy
  • AI Providers (Groq, Mistral, Gemini, etc.): Your resume and job description text is sent to these providers for AI processing. Data is not retained by these providers beyond the request.
  • Railway (Backend Hosting): Our backend API is hosted on Railway. Request logs may be retained for up to 7 days for debugging.

5. Data Retention

Resume and job description data sent to our API is processed in memory and not permanently stored. Anonymous device IDs and usage counts are stored in our database indefinitely to maintain usage limits, but contain no personally identifiable information.

6. Children's Privacy

CraftCV is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your anonymous device data by contacting us. Since we do not collect personal identifiers, we cannot link device data to a specific person unless you provide your device ID.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We follow industry best practices to protect your data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
